ABOUT US

Star Swirl is a jewelry brand. Each piece is a mix of artistic skill, the finest gemstones, and precision that even radiates luxury and sophistication in symphony for those seeking the extraordinary.

During early 2022, Mycah Aquino was looking through the night sky with disappointment. As usual with the Philippines, the smoke covered the beauty of the night sky. As she decided to retreat to her bedroom, Mycah caught a glimpse of a pendant she bought from an expensive brand. It was a silver star necklace embedded with diamonds. Though expensive, the necklace struck Mycah as rather plain. "I can do better," she thought.

Later that night she started sketching designs of necklaces and other jewelry that made her feel even more close to the sky that hides behind the smoke and clouds.
